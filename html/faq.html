What is the main theme of the RACE?

Our main theme will be what supply security means for Europe. Through the RACE, participants can explore how Europe can secure raw materials supply for the energy transition. Through working together on challenges participants will investigate how we can secure the supply chain of Raw Materials in Europe through entrepreneurship and innovation.

When is the event happening ?

The RACE 2023 will take place in person starting on the 22nd July and ending on the 5th August 2023. Once you register and your application is approved, we will send you all the details needed for the next stage. You will have access to all event activities and networking opportunities.

Can I arrive and/or depart earlier and/or later than the event start and/or end?

The 22nd July and 5th August are travel days so you must arrive and depart on these days. You cannot arrive any later or leave any earlier, as you will risk disrupting the programme. Accommodation is only covered from 22 July - 5 August.

I want to apply as a Racer – what should I know?

Join the RACE 2023 as a Racer if you want to learn & innovate! Get access to:

  • Expert lecturers
  • Workshops
  • Site visits
  • HR sessions
  • Entrepreneurship training

Join this track if you are:

  • A student currently enrolled in an EIT-Labelled or EIT RawMaterials Partner university MSc programme with 1 year complete

You need to be proficient in written and spoken English and have a background and/or expertise in Earth Sciences, Environmental Sciences, Sustainability, Material Science, Materials Recycling, Industrial Ecology, Environmental Management and Policy, Mining, Metallurgy, Business, Economics or similar.
